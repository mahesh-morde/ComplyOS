Regulations are code.

Why Agentic AI? Because regulations require logic, reasoning, and context—not just keyword searches. ComplyOS bridges the gap between legal semantics and operational execution, treating regulatory texts as programmable workflows.

Real-world Scenario

The Compliance Journey

1. Circular Released & Detected

ComplyOS ingests a new SEBI circular immediately upon publication.

2. Obligation Extraction

Agents extract specific obligations and identify affected internal teams (e.g., IT, Risk).

3. Task Creation & Assignment

Compliance tasks are auto-generated and routed to responsible owners with deadlines.

4. Audit Trail Generation

As teams complete tasks, evidence is collected to generate a complete compliance report.
